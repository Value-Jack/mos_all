Evaluation of Synthetic Speech (Naturalness and Similarity)

The purpose of this study is to learn how to improve synthetic speech. Please listen to the audio files below by clicking on the icons.

For each audio, please rate Naturalness and Similarity from 1 to 5. Please listen with headphones.

Naturalness means how much the audio sounds like a real human voice. Similarity means how well the audio matches the appearance and speaking style implied by the face image and text prompt. Do not judge grammar or sentence content.

Listening check

First, listen to this audio and write what you heard. This is required.
